RECOMMENDATIONS:

What do i bring?

You must have a front and rear light, and a helmet. Obviously you will need your bicycle. If you do not have one, you can borrow one from a team member or friend. For your bicycle we suggest you also bring spare tubes, tires, pump, tools, and patches depending on your riding style. Don't forget that helmet. Your stay with us is going to span a full 24 hours: we suggest that you consider bringing some of the following:

-lawn chairs
-food and water
-blanket
-your gameboy
-energy drinks
-power bars!
-money (beer anyone?)
-camera
-good cheers
-etc....

and again:

-bike tubes
-tires
-patch kit
-extra tubes
-tire pump
-bike lock
-helmet
-rear and front light

Safety:

Helmets prevent huge, gaping head injuries.Law requires front and rear lights at night. While we recommend you obey this law, it is not mandatory as we have no control over what you do as an independent rider.

Laws:

We also recommend that you follow all traffic laws. A bike is a vehicle under GA laws are you are required to follow them, but it is a relay...

Parking:

The gravel lot next to the A&P lofts (881 Memorial Dr SE) is available for parking. This parking is at your own risk, as it is unmonitored. We suggest you drop off everything you will need for the relay at 97 Estoria and park your car at the gravel lot or at home, then ride your bicycle back to 97 Estoria.
