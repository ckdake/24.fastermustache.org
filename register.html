what you're in for

FM.24.06 is a 24 hour relay through the streets of Atlanta. Gather a team of 4-6 riders and pass the baton through grueling traffic, up insane hills, and through dark alleys to complete laps for prizes. Faster Mustache, in conjunction with Johnny's Pizza and a host of sponsors, offers the most exciting weekend of urban cycling of the year and a chance to be crowned the king or queen of the streets!

register

You may register as a solo rider or as a team of 4 to 6. Registration up until October 31st is $10 a person. Registration after October 31st including on site is $15 a person. The waiver is available and must be submitted in person at the beginning of the race. If you register, we will e-mail you when they are available. To register, send an email to 24@fastermustache.org with "FM24 Registration" in the subject line. Include:

  • Your Team Name
  • Your name, your phone number, and your email address
  • The name, phone number, and email address of everyone else on your team
  • How you are paying
Make sure you get a confirmation email from me! If you don't, send me another email. The prefered method of payment is a cash delivery to one of the race organizers (for now ckdake is keeping track of this), but if you feel the need or are from out of town, feel free to use paypal to pay 24@fastermustache.org. If you use paypal, please add 50 cents to each person due to paypal fees.
