THE ROUTE

The ride starts at 97 Estoria (a cabbagetown bar) and makes a 11.5 mile loop through the peachtree corridor and several Atlanta neighborhoods. The route takes approximately 45 min at a casual, non-stop pace. All riders will be expected to follow this route as shown on the honor system, they will also be required to pick up lap tickets at the designated spots.

This route will take the rider through some notable Atlanta hotspots including a long stretch down Atlanta's central artery. The route includes a variety of urban street riding conditions including quiet neighborhoods, large hills, dense traffic, and commercial routes which will test the riders ability in the Atlanta cycling environment.
