RULES AND LAP MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

RULES:

GENERAL
1. all riders will follow the route shown on the back of their spoke card. this is the official route and must be respected unless there is a complete blockage and an alternate route must be taken. in this event, please contact one of the ride organizers.
2. all riders will stop at the dessignated waypoints and pick up a ticket during their route lap. these tickets must be collected in order for your lap to be counted as valid. accidentally forgetting or any other bullshit excuse will not be respected. for your lap to count, you must have 3 waypoint tickets.
3. all riders MUST check-in at the end of each lap. the rider may then continue on or tade off with a team member.
4. before you leave the check-in to ride a lap, you must make it apparent to the current staff that you are leaving for an official lap and not for a casual cruise around town. the current staff will be keeping track of who is riding when. this is a safety percaution.
5. all riders MUST display their team number on their body or bike at all times while riding
6. each team may only have one rider completeing laps at any given time
7. dont try to cheat. its not cool. much of this is on the honor system. you will be disqualified without refund or free food if you are found cheating.
8. if you see another rider has been hit or is injured you MUST stop to help. this may be a relay but each rider is more important than your pride.

OTHER
1. each rider will register by 11:00am and will have signed the liability waiver and paid in full. NOTE - ALL RIDERS DO NOT HAVE TO BE PRESENT AT THE TIME OF THE BEGINNING OF THE RACE, BUT THEY MUST HAVE PAID AND SIGNED THE LIABILITY RELEASE FORM
2. each team will choose their first rider, and have him/her ready to ride at 11:30am
3. all participants not riding are welcome to remain at 97 Estoria. if you piss off the employees at 97 Estoria and are asked to leave their property, you will be disqualified from the relay.
4. all participants will abide to the requests of the 97 Estoria staff.
5. you may not park a car and leave it for 24 consecutive hours in the 97 Estoria parking lot. additional parking is avaliable in the A&P lofts lot just a few blocks away.

LAP MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

The lap management system we are using consists of various waypoints along the route in which you will be required to get off your bike, open a "newspaper dispenser" and take a ticket. There will be a combination lock on the dispenser to prevent non-participants from tampering with the relay. You will take a ticket from each waypoint and at the end of every lap you will drop them off at the check-in table. Do not try and take more than one ticket. The numbering on the tickets allows us to track each person's actions and any gap in the numbering will show that you have cheated. So at the end of every lap, you will have several tickets, one of each color, to hand in at the staff table. The locations of the dispensers and the combination codes to get in them will be avaliable on the back of your spoke card.

We understand that there are many routes you could possibly take to reach the various waypoints. Because there is no waypoint in a certain location does not mean you may create your own route. YOU MUST ALWAYS FOLLOW THE GIVEN ROUTE. This is based on the honor system, and if you see somone cheating, please let the staff know. This is a friendly, community building event and we have no room for dishonest assbuckets.

Thats it for now. All this information and more will be on an information sheet you will get at registration time. Please email with questions/comments.
